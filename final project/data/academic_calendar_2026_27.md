# Academic Calendar for 2026-2027

Source: IIT Bombay Academic Office
URL: https://acad.iitb.ac.in/sites/default/files/Academic_Calendar_2026-27_FINAL.pdf

## Instructions Pertaining to Academic Activities

1. Many academic units organize orientation programmes for Teaching Assistants (TAs) and R&D orientations for new PG and UG entrants. An Institute level orientation is also often organized for these purposes. Attendance and participation in the orientation programme is mandatory for TAs. Absence from the same will make the TAs liable for a financial penalty in scholarship/financial support.

2. Registration for second half-semester courses should also be completed at the beginning of the semester.

3. The presentation and submission of grade for the credit seminar must be completed by the last date of grade submission as per the Academic Calendar.

4. Saturdays are included in both mid-semester and end-semester examination dates.

5. Grades for NPTEL/SWAYAM to be submitted during online grade submission window (if opted for).

6. Academic Office registers students for Semester Exchange courses as and when student returns from foreign institutes.

7. The classes missed due to public holidays may be compensated on Saturdays (before 05:30 p.m.) with an intimation to Dean (AP), subject to availability of classroom.

8. Students completing their academic graduation requirement from 27 Feb 2026 (Friday) to 5 Aug 2026 (Wednesday) (tentative) shall be included in the 64th Convocation to be held in August 2026.

9. Students completing their academic graduation requirement from 6 Aug 2026 (Thursday) (tentative) to the date of Senate meeting to be held in February 2027 shall be included in the Spring Session of the 65th Convocation to be held on 27 Feb 2027 (Saturday).

## Project / Dissertation / Ph.D Annual Progress Seminar Schedule

For Ph.D. (APS/BPS) students, the Semester Progress Report is submitted one week before the Presentation/Assessment date. For Autumn semester this runs from the first day of registration of the Autumn Semester up to 30 Sep 2026 (Wednesday), with presentation/assessment by 2 Oct 2026 (Friday). For Spring semester, submission runs up to 26 Feb 2027 (Friday), with presentation/assessment by 4 Mar 2027 (Thursday).

For project/dissertation/credit seminar related presentations, the report/dissertation should be submitted at least seven days prior to the date of presentation/assessment.

## Academic Committee Meetings

UGPC/PGPC/UGPGPC meetings (tentative, usually on a Wednesday) are held two to three weeks prior to the Senate Meeting. PGAPEC meetings are usually held on the last Wednesday of every month. Academic Support Programme (ASP) Committee meetings and Academic Performance Review by UGAPEC/PGAPEC are held after the last date of Academic Activities for the semester.
